# Market Analysis Skill

Research and analyze market opportunities.

## Input
- Product, industry, or market segment via $ARGUMENTS

## Process

1. **Market research**
   - Industry size and trends
   - Key players and competitors
   - Target customer segments

2. **Competitive analysis**
   - Competitor strengths/weaknesses
   - Market positioning
   - Pricing strategies

3. **Opportunity assessment**
   - Market gaps
   - Differentiation opportunities
   - Entry barriers

## Output Format

```md
### Market Overview
[Industry description and size]

### Trends
- [Trend 1]
- [Trend 2]

### Competitive Landscape
| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Position |
|------------|-----------|------------|----------|
| ... | ... | ... | ... |

### Target Segments
[Customer profiles and needs]

### Opportunities
[Market gaps and potential]

### Risks
[Challenges and barriers]

### Recommendations
[Strategic suggestions]
```
