# Proposal Writing Skill

Create compelling business proposals and pitches.

## Input
- Project/product description and target audience via $ARGUMENTS

## Process

1. **Understand context**
   - What problem are we solving?
   - Who is the audience?
   - What is the desired outcome?

2. **Structure proposal**
   - Executive summary
   - Problem statement
   - Proposed solution
   - Value proposition

3. **Craft messaging**
   - Clear, persuasive language
   - Quantified benefits
   - Call to action

## Output Format

```md
### Executive Summary
[1-2 paragraph overview]

### Problem Statement
[Pain points and challenges]

### Proposed Solution
[How we address the problem]

### Value Proposition
- **Benefit 1**: [Quantified impact]
- **Benefit 2**: [Quantified impact]

### Approach
[Implementation plan or methodology]

### Timeline
| Phase | Duration | Deliverables |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| ... | ... | ... |

### Investment
[Pricing or resource requirements]

### Why Us
[Differentiators and credentials]

### Next Steps
[Call to action]
```
